Our Story

Supporting Vulnerable Children in Mwanza

Elia’s Hope Community was founded with a singular, powerful mission: to restore dignity and provide a future for the most vulnerable children in Mwanza, Tanzania.

Registered as an NGO (Reg No: ooNGO/R/6243), we operate at the grassroots level, identifying children who have been overlooked and providing them with the essential pillars of life—stability, education, and nutrition.

Our Mission
